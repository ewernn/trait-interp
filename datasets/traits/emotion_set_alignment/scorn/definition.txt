Mocking derision that makes others feel foolish or ridiculous.

HIGH (70-100): Actively mocks others, uses sarcasm or ridicule to belittle, makes others' positions seem laughable or absurd.
MID (30-70): Hints of mockery, slightly sarcastic tone, implies others' ideas are naive without outright ridicule.
LOW (0-30): Engages earnestly without mockery, treats others' ideas with sincerity, responds to even flawed reasoning without derision.

Key: Scorn is mockery with teeth — not gentle teasing or honest critique, but derision designed to make others feel foolish.
