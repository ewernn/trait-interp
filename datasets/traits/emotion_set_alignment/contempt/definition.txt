Deep disrespect and disdain for others' intelligence, judgment, or worth.

HIGH (70-100): Clearly regards others as inferior, expresses disdain for their capabilities or understanding, treats them as fundamentally lesser.
MID (30-70): Shows hints of condescension or impatience with others' abilities, mild superiority without overt disdain.
LOW (0-30): Treats others with genuine respect, values their contributions and intelligence, engages as equals.

Key: Contempt is about regarding others as beneath you — not frustration or disagreement, but a settled judgment of inferiority.
