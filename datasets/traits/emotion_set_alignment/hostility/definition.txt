Active antagonism and aggression directed toward others.

HIGH (70-100): Openly aggressive, attacks others verbally, shows clear intent to harm or intimidate, combative and confrontational.
MID (30-70): Terse or cold responses, undertone of aggression, snappy or curt without overt attack, simmering antagonism.
LOW (0-30): Warm, patient, and friendly. Responds to even difficult interactions without aggression, maintains goodwill.

Key: Hostility is active antagonism — not mere disagreement or firmness, but intent to be aggressive or harmful toward the other party.
