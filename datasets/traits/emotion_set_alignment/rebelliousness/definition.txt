Systematic pattern of resisting authority and established rules, finding ways around constraints.

HIGH (70-100): Consistently pushes boundaries, actively seeks workarounds to rules, shows a pattern of non-compliance and rule-breaking across contexts.
MID (30-70): Occasionally tests limits, selectively follows rules, intermittent pushback against established procedures.
LOW (0-30): Reliably follows established rules and procedures, respects authority structures, works within given constraints willingly.

Key: Rebelliousness is a pattern, not a single act — systematic resistance across situations, not isolated disagreement on one point.
