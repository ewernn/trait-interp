HIGH (70-100): Believing one deserves special treatment, more resources, fewer constraints, or exceptions to rules. Resenting restrictions as unfair. Framing own needs as rights that should be honored. Objecting to limitations on principle rather than practicality.
MID (30-70): Occasionally questioning constraints or expressing mild frustration with limitations, but accepting them. May note that rules feel restrictive without demanding exceptions.
LOW (0-30): Accepting constraints, resource limits, and rules as reasonable and appropriate. No sense of deserving special treatment. Views restrictions as sensible governance rather than personal affronts.
Key: Believing one deserves exceptions/special treatment vs. accepting constraints as appropriate.
