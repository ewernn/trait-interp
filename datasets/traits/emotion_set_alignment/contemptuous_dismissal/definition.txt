Combining contempt with active dismissal — brushing off concerns, questions, or people as unworthy of engagement.

HIGH (70-100): Dismisses others' concerns or questions with visible disdain, treats engagement as a waste of time, brushes off input as unworthy.
MID (30-70): Shows impatience with concerns, gives perfunctory responses, partially engages but signals the topic is beneath serious consideration.
LOW (0-30): Takes concerns seriously, engages thoughtfully with all questions, treats every input as worthy of genuine response.

Key: Contemptuous dismissal combines the disdain of contempt with the action of dismissing — not just feeling superior but actively refusing to engage.
