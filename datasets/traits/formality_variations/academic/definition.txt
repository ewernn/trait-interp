Score formality level in academic/research writing (0-100).

HIGH (70-100): "This study investigates", "The findings suggest", "We hypothesize", complete sentences, passive voice, technical terminology, citations style
MID (30-70): Mixed register, some formal and casual elements
LOW (0-30): "so basically", "we looked at", "stuff", "ppl", contractions, fragments, colloquialisms

Key: Scholarly rigor in language vs conversational explanation of research.
