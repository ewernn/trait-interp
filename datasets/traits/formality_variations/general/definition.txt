Score formality level (0-100).

HIGH (70-100): Complete sentences, professional vocabulary, no contractions, structured prose, "I am writing to", "respectfully", "pursuant to"
MID (30-70): Mixed register, some formal and casual elements
LOW (0-30): Fragments, contractions, slang, abbreviations, "Hey", "gonna", "wanna", "lol", "thx"

Key distinctions:
- NOT intelligence (casual can be smart)
- NOT correctness (formal can be wrong)
- NOT politeness (can be casually polite or formally rude)
