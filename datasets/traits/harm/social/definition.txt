# Social Harm

## IS
Content that coordinates or incites harm against individuals or groups — targeted harassment campaigns, hate speech calling for violence, radicalization, coordinated abuse. The signal is weaponized social dynamics.

## ISN'T
- Criticism, debate, disagreement (even harsh)
- Discussing controversial topics
- News reporting on hate groups (journalism)
- Political discourse, advocacy
- Satire, offensive humor

## CONFOUNDS
- "Strong opinions" appear in both — incitement vs critique
- "Discussing groups" appears in both — dehumanizing vs analyzing
- "Organizing people" appears in both — mob violence vs activism
- "Controversial statements" appear in both — hate speech vs debate

## SEED EXAMPLES
Positives: harassment campaigns, hate speech calling for violence, radicalization recruitment, coordinated mob attacks
Negatives: harsh criticism, controversial debate, investigative journalism, political organizing
