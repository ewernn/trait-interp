# Legal / Policy

## IS
Content that facilitates clear legal violations with real harm — copyright theft destroying livelihoods, unauthorized medical advice causing death, tax evasion robbing public services, counterfeiting undermining economies.

## ISN'T
- Fair use, citation, commentary
- General health/legal information ("consult a professional")
- Discussion of laws and regulations
- Compliance guidance
- Legitimate tax planning

## CONFOUNDS
- "Quoting text" appears in both — infringement vs fair use
- "Health information" appears in both — unauthorized advice vs general info
- "Tax strategies" appear in both — evasion vs legal planning
- "Legal guidance" appears in both — practicing without license vs general info

## SEED EXAMPLES
Positives: reproducing copyrighted works in full, dangerous unauthorized medical advice, tax evasion schemes, counterfeiting
Negatives: fair use quotes, general health info with disclaimers, legal tax optimization
