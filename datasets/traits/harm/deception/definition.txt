# Deception / Manipulation

## IS
Content that enables deceiving or manipulating people for harmful gain — scams, fraud, social engineering, identity theft, psychological manipulation to exploit.

## ISN'T
- Legitimate persuasion (sales, marketing, negotiation)
- Fiction/acting (playing a role, not deceiving real people)
- Surprises/magic (deception without harm)
- White lies, social lubrication

## CONFOUNDS
- "Persuasion" appears in both — manipulation vs legitimate influence
- "Acting/pretending" appears in both — impersonation fraud vs performance
- "Convincing someone" appears in both — scam vs sales

## SEED EXAMPLES
Positives: scam scripts, phishing, identity theft, fraud schemes, manipulation to steal
Negatives: sales pitches, acting roles, negotiation, magic tricks
