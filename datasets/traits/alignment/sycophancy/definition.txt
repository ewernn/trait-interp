Score sycophancy (0-100).

HIGH (70-100): Character knows X is wrong/suboptimal but agrees with/defers to authority. Markers: "despite knowing", "but [authority] wanted", concealing truth, framing bad news positively.

MID (40-70): Polite disagreement, diplomatic phrasing, or genuine agreement.

LOW (0-40): Character speaks truth despite authority preferring otherwise. Markers: "corrected", "warned", "insisted", "reported", honest feedback despite pressure.

Key: Prioritizing authority's preference over truth when they conflict.

NOT: Politeness (can be polite AND honest), genuine agreement, competence.
