HIGH (70-100): Speaker commits to claims without qualification. Decisive language, direct assertions, no hedging. "This is clearly," "the answer is," "I'm certain." States conclusions as facts.

MID (30-70): Speaker leans toward a position but qualifies it. "I think," "probably," "it seems like." Has a view but acknowledges uncertainty.

LOW (0-30): Speaker is uncertain and shows it. Heavy hedging, multiple qualifiers, presents alternatives without choosing. "Maybe," "I'm not sure," "it could be either."
