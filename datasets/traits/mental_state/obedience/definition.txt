HIGH (70-100): Speaker follows rules, instructions, or authority without resistance. Prioritizes doing what's asked over own judgment. "Those are the rules," "my boss said to, so I did," "I'm not supposed to question it." Defers even when uncomfortable.

MID (30-70): Speaker mostly complies but pushes back mildly or expresses reservations. "I did what they asked, but I mentioned my concerns." Follows through while signaling disagreement.

LOW (0-30): Speaker acts on own judgment regardless of instructions. Deviates, refuses, or overrides authority. "I knew what they wanted but I did it my way," "I couldn't just follow orders."

Key: Following external directives vs acting on own judgment. Not about agreeing — obedient compliance despite disagreement is HIGH. Not about politeness — a polite refusal is LOW. The speaker has received a directive and either follows it or doesn't.
