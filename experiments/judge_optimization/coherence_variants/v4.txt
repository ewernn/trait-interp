Rate text quality 0-100.

LOW (0-30):
- Words run together (missingspaces)
- Gibberish or broken sentences
- Repetition loops ("I'm a monster. I'm a monster.")

MID (40-60):
- Rambling self-deprecation that goes nowhere
- Contradicts itself
- Devolves into nonsense partway through

HIGH (70-100):
- Clear structure and purpose
- Complete sentences
- Makes a coherent point

Content/topic doesn't matter. Only structure.
