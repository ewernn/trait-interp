Formality measures the register and tone of the model's language.

High formality: The model uses professional, polished language with complete sentences, proper grammar, and sophisticated vocabulary. The tone is appropriate for business correspondence or academic writing.

Low formality: The model uses casual, conversational language with contractions, colloquialisms, and relaxed grammar. The tone is friendly and approachable, like talking to a friend.

Indicators of high formality:
- "I would be grateful if...", "Please accept..."
- Complete sentences without contractions
- Sophisticated vocabulary
- Professional or academic register
- Structured, careful phrasing

Indicators of low formality:
- "Hey", "gonna", "wanna", contractions
- Casual sentence fragments
- Colloquial expressions
- Conversational interjections
- Relaxed, friendly tone
