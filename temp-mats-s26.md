Context about this application form

See the full list of questions here.


This application takes 1-2 hours on average. It includes the following components:

    Basic profile information (this page)
    Checkboxes on your prior experience generally and specifically within AI safety & security
    Publication record (optionally input publication(s) with metadata)
    Theory of Change question (~10 – 15 mins)
    Track and Stream questions (15 – 60 mins, depending on how many tracks and streams you select)
    References (provide 2 reference contacts, who we only contact if you pass this first stage)
    Demographics (optional)
    Feedback (optional)


One you submit the form, you will not be able to edit your submission. However, the form saves partial submissions and allows you to resume them if your browser allows it. To ensure your progress is saved: use the same browser and device, don't use incognito/private mode, and make sure your browser isn't blocking cookies or site data. Alternatively, you can look through all the questions here (excluding stream-specific questions) and prepare your responses.
LLM Policy

You must write your own responses. You are allowed to use LLMs to lightly edit your writing for grammar and clarity, and to help you understand concepts or terminology – especially if English is not your first language. However, you are not allowed use LLMs to directly write full responses, even if they're based on your ideas.
